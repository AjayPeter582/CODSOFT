Fitstyle pro

FitStyle Pro is an extension which creates personalized avatars from the provided sizing information of the user to visualize the dress they are purchasing online. Our project uses an AI based personalized avatar on online dress shopping platforms to improve the accuracy of sizing information, enhance visualization of dress fit, reduce refund and exchange process and provide personalized style recommendations. These personalized avatars empower users to make confident, user-friendly and informed decisions when purchasing dresses online which also provides a competitive advantage in the market.
